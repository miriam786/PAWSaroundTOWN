ABOUT

WHAT WE DO



Paws about town aims to offer Londoners a hub for eateries that celebrate all things animal. We started off as a blog wanting to collate dog-friendly cafes and restaurants in the city, but have since expanded our research and reviews to cafes that also host animals, for those of us who aren't able to keep pets but love giving out cuddles, and cafes and restaurants that celebrate animals and pets through their food. All within the M25.

We only support cafes who host animals that have been rehomed or adandoned and sheltered by the owners, as we believe that this is the most ethical way and only appropriate way in which we might be able to enjoy the company of animals that fund a kind of business. You can read more about this on Our ethos page.

We aim to give back to these animal supporting restaurants through our partnership The Kennel Club, a rewards system that offers it's subscribers amazing deals at our partnering restaurants, with atleast 50% of all profits made through the system going to a animal rights charity of the restaurants choice. The Kennel log-in page for members can be accessed here, and you can read up on more benefits on the page.

If you have any questions for us, or are interested in finding out more, you can check out our FAQ's. Alternatively, if something is missing or you can't find the answer to your question, feel free to contact us using the form below, or at one of our social media sites.
